What is Stockify?

Stockify is a comprehensive web application designed to help users efficiently manage their stock portfolios. Whether you are an individual investor or a business managing multiple stock assets, Stockify provides a user-friendly interface to track, update, and manage stock information.

Key Features

  • CRUD Operations: Easily create, read, update, and delete stock entries.
  • Flash messages: Flash messages are shown for confirmation after the user performs an action
  • Data Management: Store and manage detailed stock information, including name, ticker symbol, and price.
  • Responsive Design: Access Stockify from any device with a modern, responsive web design.
  • Pagination: Efficiently view and manage large lists of stocks with pagination features.
  • User-friendly and efficient:The application well-structured and easy to navigate.
